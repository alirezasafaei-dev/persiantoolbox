# Off-Page SEO & Link Building Strategy — PersianToolbox.ir

**Date:** 2026-07-22
**Goal:** Increase Domain Authority through quality backlinks from Persian ecosystem

---

## Core Principles

1. **Real value for Iranian users** — Free, useful tools are the best link bait
2. **Focus on Persian ecosystem** — 99%+ traffic from Iran
3. **Quality over quantity** — Natural, relevant links from authoritative Persian domains

---

## 6-Month Targets

| Target                | Metric                                      |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Referring domains     | 80–120 unique domains                       |
| Links to tool pages   | 40%+ to tools (not just homepage)           |
| Anchor text diversity | Max 30% brand, rest partial/generic         |
| Relevant domain links | 50%+ from accounting, legal, tech, business |
| Growth rate           | 10–20 quality domains per month             |

---

## Strategy 1: Content-Driven Link Building (Highest Priority)

Create reference-quality guides that become link targets:

- "راهنمای کامل محاسبه تاخیر تأدیه چک در سال ۱۴۰۵ + ماشین‌حساب"
- "بهترین روش‌های OCR فارسی رایگان + مقایسه ابزارها"
- "چگونه آدرس فارسی را استاندارد به انگلیسی تبدیل کنیم"
- "محاسبه حقوق خالص و ناخالص ۱۴۰۵ با تمام کسورات"

## Strategy 3: Targeted Outreach

### Target Categories

- Accounting & financial blogs
- Legal & law firm sites (check damages, mahr)
- Tech & developer communities
- Startup directories & product listings
- Educational & university sites

### Outreach Process (Weekly)

1. Identify 10–15 new target sites
2. Read their content
3. Send personalized message
4. Follow up after 7–10 days
5. Track and analyze results

---

## Strategy 4: Community & Forum Presence

- Helpful answers in forums and Telegram groups
- Natural tool mentions when relevant
- Presence in "recommended resources" sections

---

## Strategy 5: Digital PR & Brand Mentions

- Send news about new tools to Persian tech media
- Collaborate with accounting, legal, freelancing influencers
- Participate in expert discussions

---

## Red Lines

- ❌ Buying links or PBN
- ❌ Excessive link exchanges
- ❌ Over-optimized anchor text
- ❌ Links from irrelevant or spam sites
- ❌ Comment spam and forum spam

---

## Timeline

**Month 1:** Prepare link-worthy pages + outreach to 20–30 sites + publish 1–2 comprehensive guides
**Month 2–3:** Expand outreach + build visual assets (infographics) + track initial results
**Month 4–6:** Scale successful processes + focus on higher-authority sites + analyze link impact

---

## Tracking

- New referring domains per month
- Dofollow vs nofollow ratio
- Anchor text distribution
- Impression/click increases on target pages after receiving links
- Domain authority growth
- Lost links and reasons
